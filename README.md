## 🚀 How to JOIN SQUAD

**Super Easy - No Discord!**

1. Click **Discussions** tab on top 👆
2. Click **New Discussion** > **Welcome - Introduce Yourself**
3. Write: Your Name, What you build, Why you join
4. Click **Start Discussion** — YOU ARE IN!

You need GitHub account = Auto Register & Login ✅
